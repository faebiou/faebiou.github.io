Since sketching and brainstorming is my favourite part of every project, I thought I'd spend my free time giving graphic design advice sometimes.

So if you already have something I'd gladly take a look and give feedback – or I could help out with getting started.

The Basics

If you would like to work with me then feel free to reach out over instagram or through an e-mail with the following things:

  • your brief, in whatever format you have it in;
  • if this is not yet clear, then paste in any information from your client, so that we may use that as starting point in trying to figure out what they want;
  • also, attach any visual cues you are able to share – whether if it's an existing sketch, inspirational references, or moodboards;

Having these things in advance makes up for a smooth and highly efficient session.

Included

A private call with me, over the platform of your choosing where screen sharing is possible.

This lasts for one entire hour, through which we are working together in trying to find the appropriate solution to your problem. I will be sketching digitally or on paper, using whatever references you have sent me earlier.

At the end of the call, you will receive this file, with all my comments, sketches, and whatever suggestions we have discussed earlier.

Not included

If we are working with vectors or code, then the process is very similar, but you will not receive my working file, because you are hiring me as a consultant, not as a graphic designer.

Of course, further collaboration is also possible – but my hourly rate will be different.

Costs

My hourly rate currently is €20 – this may vary in function of my current workload which you will pay after our meeting and will receive a receipt. There are no discounts – unless it's a school assignment.

Students

In case you are stuck with a school assignment, need feeback with it – or you think I might be able to help out, then let me know and – if I have the time for it – I'll respond pro bono.
